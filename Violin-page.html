Violin Page

The violin i have is a 1946 french violin and its sound was deep and rich and i have played multiple concerts with this violin and it had been amazing what its capable of. My favorite place to perform is at Austria, Vienna's golden hall or known as musikverein. The rich sound is magnificient.

I was also lucky enough to perform in The Pantheon in Rome, Italy. A building still standing over 2000 years old. We were very fortunate to have Mastreo Maxim Eskenasky My favorite concert was the one in Vienna, Austria Musikveirn(The Golden Hall). It was my favorit because of the rich sound and the magnificient performance.

The violin has had a pretty big impact on me it has made me a better violin player and a better person and just enjoy what I'm doing. Doing this Spring season I bought a new violin case called Musafia and it's a handmade Italian case. It looks very cool and I love it, but the downside is it is pretty heavy and not convenient for traveling.

I have been in Irvine Classical Players for over 2 and a half years and I have been studying violin with Dr. Popa in the time in the orchestra. A few things I like about Irvine classical players is that I get very unique opportunities while still having a solo even if you're not that good. For example, I went to Vienna Austria's Golden Hall (Musikverein) and I performed Czardas as my solo with my orchestra which has been in that time a very small orchestra with only 10 people. This orchestra has been very helpful to me and I have enjoyed it in the year 2024 I will be going to Nairobi, Kenya to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the European Union and UK, England. I will be performing Zigunerwisen by Sarasate and moto perpetuo by paganini as my solos.
